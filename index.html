14th May Update - Personal Sailing is now permitted

We are pleased to confirm that members can sail at Banbury Sailing Club, in line with Government guidelines.

There are restrictions, so members should refer to the e-mail they have received on what is permitted and remain socially distanced from other members and reservoir users at all times
